📜 Server Rules

Play fair.
Be kind. Have fun.

These rules apply to all players on Cloudrend SMP — in-game and in chat. Ignorance is not an excuse.

🎮

In-Game Rules

How to behave in the world

1
No Griefing Bannable

Do not destroy, modify, or steal from other players' builds without their permission. This includes farms, bases, and any placed structures.

2
No Hacking / Cheating Bannable

The use of X-ray resource packs, fly hacks, auto-clickers, or any cheat client is strictly prohibited. Play fair or don't play.

3
Respect Personal Space Warning

Do not build within [X] blocks of another player's base unless you are teammates or have explicit permission from them.

4
No Lag Machines Warning

Avoid building massive redstone clocks or mob farms that intentionally slow down the server. Keep your farms optimized and reasonable.

5
PvP Consent Warning

Do not kill other players unless both parties agree to a fight, or you are in a designated PvP zone. Surprise killing is not allowed.

💬

Chat Rules

How to talk to each other

1
Be Respectful Bannable

No harassment, bullying, or hate speech of any kind. Treat every player with basic human decency regardless of who they are.

2
No Spamming Kick

Avoid flooding the chat with repetitive messages, excessive caps lock, or random characters. Keep it readable.

3
Language Policy Warning

Keep the chat family-friendly. Avoid excessive swearing or inappropriate language — this server is open to all ages.

4
No Advertising Warning

Do not promote other servers or post website links without prior staff approval. Unauthorized ads will result in a warning or mute.

5
No NSFW Bannable

Any NSFW topics, explicit content, or inappropriate discussions are strictly prohibited — no exceptions.

6
Staff Privacy Warning

Do not argue with staff members in public chat. If you have an issue, use private messages or open a ticket. Public drama helps no one.

⚠️

Consequences

Breaking any of these rules may result in a warning, kick, temporary mute, or a permanent ban depending on the severity and frequency of the violation. Staff decisions are final — appeal via Discord if you believe a ban was unjust.
